UNSM DIRECTIVE

The United Nations of Sawtooth Military (UNSM) is a structured, multi‑branch leadership organization dedicated to developing discipline, teamwork, and operational excellence. Through coordinated training and unified command, UNSM prepares members to lead with integrity and purpose.

BRANCH COMMANDS

UNSA — Army

Ground operations, tactical readiness, and disciplined leadership.

UNSN — Navy

Strategic planning, navigation, and maritime operations.

UNSCG — Coast Guard

Rescue operations, security, and rapid‑response capability.

JCS — Joint Chiefs Staff

High‑level coordination, command oversight, and organizational leadership.
